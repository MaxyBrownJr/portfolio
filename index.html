METAL GEAR RISING: REVENGEANCE

BOSS APPRECIATION

Metal Gear RAY

The tutorial boss of the game, the Metal Gear RAY is an unmanned, AI-controlled bi-pedal machine. It reaches a height of about a 70ft and modified to be heavily armed with stealth cruise missles and a powerful plasma cannon.

Despite its large size and flashy appearance, the Metal Gear RAY fight is the easiest fight boss, natural for the tutorial boss. Due to it a one-time appearance, maching tutorial boss, there are few interesting details to divulge aside from military history and such.

As the tutorial boss, the Metal Gear RAY is the first instance of the Rules of Nature generic boss theme, which generally plays for similar machine, AI-controlled bosses.
