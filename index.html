What is Metal Gear Rising: Revengeance?

Metal Gear Rising: Revengeance is a 2013 action hack and slash game developed by PlatinumGames and published by Konami Digital Entertainment. it is a spin-off in the Metal Gear series, and is set four years after the events of Metal Gear Solid 4: Guns of the Patriots. In the game, players control Raiden, a cyborg who confronts the private military company Desperado Enforcement, with the gameplay focusing on fighting enemies using a sword and other weapons to perform combos and counterattacks.
