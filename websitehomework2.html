METAL GEAR RISING: REVENGEANCE

BOSS APPRECIATION

Metal Gear RAY

The tutorial boss of the game, the Metal Gear RAY is an unmanned, AI-controlled bi-pedal machine. It reaches a height of about a 70ft and modified to be heavily armed with stealth cruise missles and a powerful plasma cannon.

Despite its large size and flashy appearance, the Metal Gear RAY fight is the easiest fight boss, natural for the tutorial boss. Due to it a one-time appearance, maching tutorial boss, there are few interesting details to divulge aside from military history and such.

As the tutorial boss, the Metal Gear RAY is the first instance of the Rules of Nature generic boss theme, which generally plays for similar machine, AI-controlled bosses.

Blade Wolf (IF Prototype LQ-84i)

The second boss of the game, Blade Wolf is a canine-like quadrupedal machine equipped with a learning AI and capable of conversation. It is equipped with a railgun, a chainsaw attached to its tail, and extendable claws.

This boss serves mainly to teach the player to perfect parry, as much of the fight can be made easier if the player is capable of doing so. Blade Wold learns through his meeting and realtionship with the player character, Raiden, to develop individuality and free will.

Blade Wolf is the first boss to have his own unique boss music, I'm My Own Master Now which lyrics represent his desire to free his expansive intelligence and capability as an AI from the company who owns and controls him.
